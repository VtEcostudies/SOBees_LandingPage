Armed with a robust assessment of the status of most wild bee species in Vermont, we have developed a conservation watch list that includes 55 native bee species. We were able to model the distribution of most bee species, allowing us to identify important habitats and areas with unique and diverse bee communities. Some of these areas we have proposed as Important Bee Areas, priority locations for protection and bee-focused land management.
